居家简易饮品

评论

「持续更新中，喝就完事了。」

!!! 内容包括含酒精饮品，未成年人请勿饮酒，酗酒有害健康 !!!

自隔离以来所有酒吧咖啡店种种全部关门，不爱喝白水的我折腾出一堆喝的玩意，现记录在此。需要器材仅为冰格、杯子和筷子一根，原材料以手头现有的凑活为主，适合居家懒癌患者。
注：本篇内容用量非常不精确，未注明的请酌情添加。1 shot = 30~45 mL

Hot

Irish coffee ★

  • Honey whiskey 1 shot
  • Agave syrup / brown sugar
  • Espresso double~triple
  • Whipped cream topping
  • (Caramel topping)
  • (Salt)
    Do not mix

Cointreau tea

  • Tea
    La Route des Arômes - L’Été Indien (Thé vert aromatisé mangue et passion, parsemé de morceaux de mangue et d’ananas, pétales d’oranges)
  • Cointreau 0.5 shot

Iced

Coke with anything

  • Whiskey / rum / or anything
  • Coke
  • Lime

Mojito

  • Rum 1 shot
  • Mint syrup 0.5 spoon
  • Lemon syrup 1~1.5 spoon
  • Lime
  • Tonic water

Gin tonic (my version)

  • Gin 1.5 shot
  • Lemon syrup 1.5~2 spoon
  • (Cold brew black tea 50 mL)
  • Tonic water / Perrier
  • (Lemon sorbet)

Grapefruit fizz ★

  • Gin 30 mL
  • Cointreau 15 mL
  • Grapefruit-orange syrup 1.5 spoon
  • Lemon syrup 0.5 spoon
  • Grenadine (press in the glass)
  • Tonic water
  • (Grapefruit slice)

Cointreau mule

  • Cointreau 1.5 shot
  • Gin 0.5 shot
  • Lime half
  • Ginger beer fill up

Whiskey mule ★

  • Honey whiskey 30 mL
  • Cointreau 15 mL
  • Lemon juice 0.5 spoon
  • Ginger beer fill to 150 mL
  • Dry ginger powder
  • Lemon slice
